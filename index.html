About Us

A project spearheaded by students and members of the George Mason University community, The Contemporary Mural Arts Festival at Mason was started as an idea to enrich and add life to the current landscape of the GMU campus. Proposed and instigated by then freshman Fionnuala Fisk, and supported closely by the Universityâ€™s Provisions Library and University Curator Donald Russell, The Contemporary Mural Arts Festival team has only grown since then. With more and more students of various backgrounds, fields of study, and class standings, the #MuralsAtMason team stands as a microcosm of what the project hopes to accomplish; bringing various community members together through the cohesive nature of public art.
